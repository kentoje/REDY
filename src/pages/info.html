FALCON 9

Falcon 9 is a two-stage-to-orbit medium lift launch vehicle, it is powered by Merlin engines, burning liquid oxygen (LOX) and rocket-grade kerosene (RP-1) propellants.. Its name is from the Millennium Falcon and the nine engines of the rocket's first stage.

MARS

Mars is the fourth planet from the Sun and the second-smallest planet in the Solar System after Mercury. Thanks to scientific investigations, we now know that human life is possible, the volume of water detected.

DOUBLE SHELL

REDHOUSE uses a unique dual-shell system to isolate the habitable spaces from the natural expansion and contraction caused by extreme temperature swings on Mars. As a result, the interior is free to be light, airy, highly mass-optimized, and human: a tiny bubble of Earth on a distant world.

3D PRINTING

Martian exploration and settlement at any meaningful scale depend on materials found on Mars. This is enabled by a technology known as in-situ resource utilization (ISRU). ISRU circumvents the limits of the rocket equation   that every 10 pounds of rocket need 90 pounds of propellant. Without ISRU, the cost of importing materials from Earth renders the project impossible.

Second, habitats are completed with life-support systems. We sended machines in advance to harvest the Martian landscape, extracting materials for robotic printers to build our first homes on Mars in anticipation of our arrival.
